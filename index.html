About Me

Hi, my name is Rogelio Mares Jr, and I am a graphic designer with 4 years of experience in creating visually impactful designs that communicate a message effectively. I have a passion for design and a talent for using color, typography, and composition to bring ideas to life. I specialize in html, css, java script, and Adobe Cloud and I approach each project with a focus on understanding the

client's needs and finding creative solutions to meet their goals. My technical skills include proficiency in Adobe Photoshop & Illustrator, and I am always looking for ways to expand my skills and knowledge. I am a reliable, detail-oriented, and collaborative designer, and I would love the opportunity to work with you on your next project.
